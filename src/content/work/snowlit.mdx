SnowLit is designed to streamline the process of research literature analysis by leveraging Scopus data. 

It provides tools for extracting metadata from scientific documents, creating detailed visualizations, and employing forward and backward snowballing techniques to discover additional research papers. 

The tool outputs data in various formats including CSV files and PPT presentations, offering insights into the distribution of papers by year, source, type, and other parameters, as well as identifying top-cited works and generating word clouds.
